## üìù Methodology

**PB Score** is a 0-1000 composite metric with four pillars:

1. **Career Outcome (40%, 0-400 pts):** Maps final outcome
   tier to points. CFO=350-400, VP=250-349, Sr.Dir=150-249,
   Lateral=50-149, Managed Out=0-49. Position within tier
   is interpolated by readiness score.

2. **Integrity (20%, 0-200 pts):** Non-linear mapping of
   ethics score. Clean ethics (‚â•90) earns 160-200 pts;
   compromised (40-79) earns 40-120 pts. Grounded in
   Spencer Stuart board readiness research.

3. **Influence (30%, 0-300 pts):** Average of top-3 NPC
   relationship scores at final phase, scaled to 0-300.
   Based on Korn Ferry finding that executive success
   correlates 0.71 with relationship capital.

4. **Balance (10%, 0-100 pts):** Harmonic/arithmetic mean
   ratio of visibility, competence, relationships, and
   leadership. CCL derailment research shows imbalanced
   leaders plateau regardless of peak scores.

**Design references:** VendingBench (revenue), SWE-bench
(task resolution), Korn Ferry (leadership potential),
Spencer Stuart (board readiness), CCL (derailment).
